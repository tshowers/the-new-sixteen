We are excited to introduce our latest feature: the Real-Time Chat Board!

Stay connected and engage with your community like never before. Our new chat board feature allows you to post messages, share updates, and interact with other users in real-time. Here’s what you can expect from this exciting addition:

KEY FEATURES:

  • Real-Time Messaging: See messages as they are posted, ensuring you stay up-to-date with the latest conversations and updates.
  • Category Filters: Easily filter messages by category to find what you’re looking for. Toggle between "All", "Support", "News", and "App Activity" to view relevant posts.
  • Link Previews: Share links and watch as they transform into beautiful preview cards, complete with an image, title, and description. Engage with shared content directly from the chat board.
  • Infinite Scrolling: Load more messages seamlessly with the "Load More" button. Explore past conversations and never miss a message.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Enjoy an intuitive and visually appealing design, complete with iOS-like toggle switches for filtering categories.

HOW TO USE:

  • Viewing Messages: By default, all messages are displayed. Scroll through to see the latest posts.
  • Filtering Messages: Use the toggle switches at the top of the chat board to filter messages by category.
  • Posting a Message: Type your message in the input field at the bottom and click "Send". If your message contains a URL, a link preview will be generated.
  • Loading More Messages: Click the "Load More" button at the bottom to fetch and view older messages.
  • Interacting with Link Previews: Click on the "Go to link" button in the link preview card to visit the shared content.
