Influence Analysis

This bubble chart represents the influence of various contacts based on their reach and engagement metrics. Each bubble's size corresponds to the level of influence, combining factors like follower count, engagement rate, and activity frequency. The chart helps in identifying key influencers within your network.

Insights

  • Positive Sentiment: Contacts who consistently interact positively are more likely to respond well to outreach efforts and can be targeted with confidence.
  • Negative Sentiment: Contacts with negative sentiment may need a different approach or further analysis to understand the root cause before being targeted.
