Average Engagement Per Contact

This bar chart displays the average engagement levels of contacts over a specified period. Engagement is measured by the frequency and type of interactions such as likes, shares, comments, and direct messages. Higher values indicate more active engagement from the contact.

Insights

  • High Engagement: Contacts with high average engagement are more responsive and active on social media. These are prime candidates for targeting since they are likely to interact with your content or offers.
  • Low Engagement: Contacts with low engagement might need different strategies or more personalized content to increase their responsiveness.
