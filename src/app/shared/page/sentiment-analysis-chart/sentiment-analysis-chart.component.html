Weekly Sentiment Score Trend

This line chart tracks the sentiment scores of social media mentions over time. Sentiment analysis assesses the positive, negative, or neutral tone of interactions and posts. Monitoring this trend helps in understanding public perception and mood towards your brand or topics of interest.

Insights

  • High Influence Contacts: Contacts with high influence scores can amplify your message due to their large following and high engagement rates. Targeting these influencers can help spread your message more effectively.
  • Low Influence Contacts: While they may not have as much reach, they can still be valuable, especially if they have high engagement rates within a smaller community.
